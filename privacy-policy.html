PRIVACY POLICY

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications and games developed by CiCi Game Studios. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information is collected when you use our applications, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information We Collect

Our applications may collect the following data:

  • Advertising ID for ad personalization purposes
  • In-game statistics (score, time, tasks completed) — stored locally on your device only, never transmitted
  • Anonymous usage data related to app performance

3. Third-Party Services

Our applications use Google AdMob advertising services. Google AdMob may collect certain data to serve personalized ads. You can review Google's privacy policy at:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Use of Data

Collected data is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Improving the application and game experience
  • Displaying advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • Detecting and resolving technical issues

5. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 13 years of age.

6. Data Security

Game statistics are stored only on your device (local storage) and are not transmitted to our servers. Advertising data is protected by Google's security infrastructure.
